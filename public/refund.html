Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 26 June 2026
Wallet recharges are generally non-refundable because the balance stays available for you to use. Where a service genuinely fails, we make it right — read the specifics below.

1. Wallet recharges

Money added to your Rudraganga wallet is intended to be spent on consultations and services. Recharges are non-refundable to the original payment method once credited, except where required by law or where a recharge failed but money was debited.

  • If your account was debited but the wallet was not credited, the amount is auto-reconciled, or refunded after verification, typically within 5–7 business days.
  • Duplicate charges for the same transaction are refunded after verification.

2. Consultations (call, video, chat)

Consultations are billed per minute from your wallet and are generally non-refundable once the session has taken place. However, we will credit your wallet back where:

  • You were charged but the session never connected (e.g. the astrologer did not join).
  • A technical fault on our side (call drop, audio/video failure) ended the session prematurely — we credit the affected minutes.
  • You were billed after the session ended due to a metering error.

Dissatisfaction with the content of a reading is not, by itself, grounds for a refund, as guidance is subjective. Raise any concern within 48 hours and we will review it fairly.

3. Pooja bookings

  • A booking can be cancelled for a full refund up to 24 hours before the scheduled time.
  • Cancellations within 24 hours may be refunded as wallet credit, minus any costs already incurred.
  • Once a pooja has been performed, it is non-refundable.

4. Store orders (gemstones, rudraksha, yantras & more)

  • Orders may be cancelled for a full refund before they are shipped.
  • Damaged, defective, or wrong items: report within 7 days of delivery with photos for a replacement or refund.
  • For hygiene and authenticity reasons, certain energised or personalised items may be non-returnable unless faulty. This is noted on the product page.

5. Gifts

Virtual gifts sent to astrologers are consumed immediately and are non-refundable.

6. How to request a refund

Use the Help & Support section in the app or our contact form, with your registered phone number and the transaction or session reference. Our team reviews each request and responds with the outcome.

7. Refund timelines

  • Wallet credit: applied immediately once approved.
  • Refund to original payment method: typically 5–7 business days after approval, depending on your bank or payment provider.

8. Contact us

Questions about a refund or cancellation? Reach us via the contact form or the app’s Help & Support section, and we’ll help.
